How to use Cryptolio?

  1. Search for coins.
  2. Click add coin to add a coin to your portfolio.
  3. Enter the amount of coins you have for each coin you added.
  4. Look at the bar chart for a proportional view of your porfolio.
  5. Click on bars in the bar chart to see a price chart for the corresponding coin.
